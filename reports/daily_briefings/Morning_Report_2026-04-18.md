**详情:** Whether language models can systematically generalize remains actively debated. We introduce a controlled synthetic environment based on shortest-path planning, a canonical composable sequential optimization problem. **详情:** Over the past year, spatial intelligence has drawn increasing attention. Therefore, in this paper, we focus on a fundamental and critical capability in spatial intelligence from a linguistic perspective: viewpoint rotation understanding (VRU).
